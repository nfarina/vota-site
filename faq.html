Vota automatically journals places you go. And you can choose whether to share those visits and your recommendations with your close friends. As a bonus, you can track your own vacations and trips and wander down memory lane.

We contact your bank using the login info you provide us and then request that we be added as a communication contact. From there, we ask to be notified by your bank every time your credit card is used. Once notifications are in place, we throw out all the login info. Forever. We don’t store your login info. If you visit your account at your bank website, you will see that behind the scenes “Vota” has been added as a contact.

Because we send you a mobile-app notification anytime your credit card is used, you can use that same info to catch any fraud such as someone using your credit card without your knowledge! No kidding. We have actually saved people from a surprising amount of fraud. We also think there may be something useful in helping you track your expenses -- let us know what you think!

Visit the Cards tab in the app. Tap the "+" button to add a new one. Tap on a card to view its details and remove it.

Yes, the same process for deleting your card (in order to change it) works to cease our categorization of your purchases.
