Vota links up with your credit card and lets you know about new transactions within seconds. We'll also identify and categorize your swipes automatically, so you don't have to wonder what that $100 charge for "SQ *UB123ZA" was on your statement weeks later.

Vota is new and growing. We currently support most credit and debit cards from the following banks:

  • Capital One
  • Chase
  • Citi
  • US Bank
  • Wells Fargo
  • Visa cards offered by many smaller banks and Credit Unions such as:
    • Advantis
    • E*Trade
    • First Republic
    • OnPoint
    • Umpqua
    • And on and on… Try yours!

Don’t have one of these cards? We recommend the CitiBank Double Cash card (and we don't even get any kickbacks for saying so):

  • CitiBank Double Cash

We contact your bank using the login info you provide us and then request that we be added as a communication contact. From there, we ask to be notified by your bank every time your credit card is used. Once notifications are in place, we throw away your login info. Forever. Unlike every other financial app out there, we don’t store your login info. If you visit your account at your bank website, you will see that behind the scenes “Vota” has been added as a contact.

Visit the Cards tab in the app. Tap the "+" button to add a new one. Tap on a card to view its details and remove it.

Yes, the same process for deleting your card (in order to change it) works to cease our categorization of your purchases.

Email contact@vota.app with any support questions.
